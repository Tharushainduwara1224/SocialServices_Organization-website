"Api Mithuro" Disaster Relief Services Organization

Ongoing Projects

Evacuation and resettlement of families in vulnerable areas such as floods, landslides and mudslides.

It is expected that a program to identify the risk zones and educate the people in those areas about the possibility of natural disasters will be implemented from September. NBRO has identified a list of 11,517 beneficiaries in 121 divisional secretariats of 14 districts.It is hoped to implement a loan relief program for the people through the banks. NDRSC is expected to manage funding and institutional coordination with government or social service welfare organizations for the resettlement programme.

Blood donation programs

Since there is a shortage of blood in many blood banks and blood transfusion centers in the country, blood donation programs are expected to be implemented for selected districts.

Construction of new sanitation facilities / improvement of existing sanitation facilities (toilets and bathing facilities)

Under this, sanitary facilities and broken buildings of 500 selected difficult schools are expected to be repaired. And intervening to meet the essential factors like water and electricity in consultation with the competent authorities.

Giving some facilities for lactating mothers and pregnant women giving.

Through this, it is hoped to implement a program to improve the nutritional status of pregnant mothers by giving them a nutritional bag and giving an allowance to low-income earners.

Facilitating and monitoring the distribution of drinking water during severe drought conditions.

Discussions are expected with the district and divisional secretaries to prepare an effective program for distribution of drinking water to the affected families. Lorry Bowser, Tractor Bowser, Water Tanker also for delivery.A program to make provisions for the construction and improvement of water sources is also expected to be launched.
