"Api Mithuro" Disaster Relief Services Organization

Completed projects

1.On 08.09.2021, the blood donation program held at the Gangarama temple in Colombo was successfully completed. For that, we received a lot of support from the local residents as well as the doctors and nurses of the Colombo National Hospital. Because of this, we were able to overcome the shortage of blood at the Borella Blood Transfusion Institute.

2.In conjunction with World Children's Day 2021.10.01, renovation programs for elderly and children's homes were held. Concert programs were held here with children and adults.

3.We did those activities to provide dry food, temporary huts and sanitary facilities to our own people who were affected by the bad weather that affected the entire island in May 2021. In Galle, Matara, Nuwara Eliya, Matale, Kegalle, Kandy and Kalutara districts, people awareness programs could be held with the Landslide Bureau officials regarding the landslide risk zones.

4.2020-2021 Census conducted for low income earners affected by economic crisis due to Covid pandemic. Through that, arrangements were made to provide a monthly stipend for a period of 3 months. We were able to provide a nutritional bag for pregnant mothers every month.

5.In the months of November and December 2021, schools conducted dengue control programs through government agencies. For that we got the help of the Sri Lanka Police, the Armed Forces and the Red Cross Organization.

6.On 28.02.2022, a program was launched to provide food nutrients for cancer patients and through this, it was possible to launch performance programs to improve their mental condition. Arrangements were made to hold a march from Kandy to Colombo for the development of Cancer Hospital. The money received through this was arranged to be credited to the Hospital Development Fund.
